Putin Apologises for Taking Ukraine Land

On Saturday Russian president Valdimir Putin made a formal statement, in front of NATO leaders, to Ukranian president Petro Poroshenko. Putin stated that he was "very sorry" and "didn't realise" what he was doing as he thought the land was 'unclaimed'. Putin is now working closley with Ukraine in an effort to rebuild the relationship, yet doesnt appear to have any plans of returning the taken land.
